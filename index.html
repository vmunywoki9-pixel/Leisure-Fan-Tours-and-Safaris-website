About Us

Enjoy Luxury and Adventure in Maasai Mara

Welcome to Leisure Fan Tours and Safaris, an exclusive collection of luxury safari cottages and wildlife explorer destinations set in the breathtaking wilderness of Maasai Mara. Located just minutes from Talek Gate, our houses offer unconditional access to the world-famous wildebeest migration and the rich wildlife of Maasai Mara.

Our fully furnished, luxury cottages give you all-inclusive and luxurious private spaces, perfect for relaxing in the stunning surroundings. Designed with your comfort in mind, each cottage is a sanctuary of elegance and tranquility.
